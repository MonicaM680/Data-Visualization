Marvel Cinematic Universe: Critical Scores vs. Audience Sentiment Over Time

Marvel's critical acclaim has declined, with Rotten Tomatoes scores falling to the mid-60s in recent releases. 'Ant-Man and the Wasp: Quantumania' has the lowest score at 46%, while 'Black Panther' leads at 96%. Audience scores range from 'Captain Marvel' at 45% to 'Spider-Man: No Way Home' and 'Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings' at 98%.

Note: Films are listed chronologically from 2008 to 2023, illustrating the evolution of Marvel’s cinematic appeal. Scores reflect Rotten Tomatoes’ critics and audience percentages, with higher scores indicating more favorable reviews.
